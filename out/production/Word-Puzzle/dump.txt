Word Generator

   Sometimes it can be useful to get your hands on a good word generator.
   For budding authors, poets and lovers of words (logophiles).

   There are 29,243 words to choose from, ranging from 1 to 16 characters
   long.

   The tool lets you generate up to 100 random words at a time. You can
   also make words plural, reverse them, and if you're a fan of bold words
   against a pretty background, click the "nice font" option.

   The words can be chosen at random from our big list, or you can select
   a specific category to refine the results.

   Additionally, there is the option to type in some text in the field at
   the end of the form, so that e.g. you can get a list of words which
   include "ing".

Main Options

Category

   The default option is to select your random word from any category.
   However, you can use the drop down list to choose from various
   categories such as food, sports, emotions etc.

Length Limit

   If you're looking for a word with a certain number of characters, the
   2nd menu option lets you specify the length. If you e.g. choose 6, then
   words up to 6 characters long will be chosen, unless you choose "Exact"
   in the 3rd drop down, in which case only words exactly 6 characters
   long will be returned.

Length Match

   If you have selected a length limit, choose whether the output is up to
   an including that length limit, or exactly that number of characters
   long.

Number Of Words

   The 4th drop down option lets you choose the number of words to
   generate. 25 are returned by default, but you can choose between 1 and
   100 words.

Start Letter

   Choose which letter you want your words to start with, using the 5th
   drop down menu.

Colourful Tick Box Options

Nice Font

   Tick to get your words in a nice white font on a colourful background.

Plural

   Sticks an "s" at the end of each word

Reverse

   Reverses each word, and optionally lets you include the original word.

Filter Text

   Use the "Includes This Text" input field to only return words
   containing the text you want.

   Have fun!
